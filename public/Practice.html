Joe's Favorite Anime

Top 3 Anime

Mob Psycho

Shigeo "Mob" Kageyama is a middle schooler with extraordinary psychic powers, which he often tries to suppress to avoid unwanted attention and maintain a normal life

Full Metal Alchemist

"Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood" follows the Elric brothers, Edward and Alphonse, as they seek the Philosopher's Stone after a failed attempt to resurrect their mother, leading to the loss of their bodies, and join the State Military to find a way to restore them.

Keep your Hands Off Eizouken!

"Keep Your Hands Off Eizouken!" follows three high school girls, Midori, Sayaka, and Tsubame, as they form an animation club to create their "ultimate world" in the fictional waterside city of Shibahama.
